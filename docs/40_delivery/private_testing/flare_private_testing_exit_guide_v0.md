# Flare Private Testing Exit Guide V0

**Operator preflight:** replace `[SUPPORT EMAIL OR CHANNEL]` and `[EXPECTED RESPONSE WINDOW]` before sending.

## Participation Is Voluntary

You may stop participating in the private test at any time.

## How To Leave The Test

1. Contact `[SUPPORT EMAIL OR CHANNEL]`.
2. Say that you want to leave the Flare private test.
3. If you want access removed, say that directly.
4. If you want help concerning private-test data, say that directly too.

## GroupMe Before Leaving

If you connected GroupMe and the build allows it, disable or disconnect that channel before leaving.

## What To Expect In V0

- Some data handling may still be manual during this private test.
- Deletion or export behavior may not yet be fully self-service.
- Leaving the test does not guarantee deletion from external providers.
- GroupMe messages already sent remain subject to GroupMe and the group's own retention behavior.
