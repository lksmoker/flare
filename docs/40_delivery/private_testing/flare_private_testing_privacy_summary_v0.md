# Flare Private Testing Privacy Summary V0

**Operator preflight:** replace `[SUPPORT EMAIL OR CHANNEL]` and `[EXPECTED RESPONSE WINDOW]` before sending.

This is a plain-language summary for the private test. It is not a formal privacy policy.

## What Account Information Flare Uses

For authenticated private-test features, Flare uses account information such as your email address and account identifier.

Authenticated features are not anonymous.

## What Configuration And Plan Information May Be Stored

Depending on what you use, Flare may store:

- your Behavior Pattern setup
- your Anchor Note content
- your Flare Plan actions and their order
- your support-channel setup status
- your saved support message preview and selected GroupMe destination details

## What Flare Event And Plan-Run Information May Be Stored

Flare may store information about Flare Events and related activity, including:

- when a Flare Event was created
- event status and attached reflection data
- plan-run status
- plan-step outcomes such as done, skipped, or not reached
- timestamps related to those actions

## What GroupMe Destination And Delivery Information May Be Stored

If you connect GroupMe, Flare may store:

- the selected destination identifier
- the selected destination display name
- connection status
- delivery attempt status
- delivery-related timestamps

## What Content Is Sent To GroupMe

When GroupMe is enabled and you send a test Flare or a real Flare, Flare sends the predefined support message for that channel to the selected GroupMe group.

Test Flare messages are meant to be clearly marked as tests. Real Flare messages are not test-labeled.

## GroupMe Has Its Own Systems

Messages sent to GroupMe are separately processed and retained under GroupMe's own systems, policies, membership controls, and notification behavior.

Leaving this private test does not guarantee deletion from GroupMe or from the devices of people in that group.

## Provider Tokens And Secrets

Provider tokens are intended to remain backend-controlled. They should not be intentionally exposed to the client.

## Important Limits On Privacy Claims

This private test does not claim:

- end-to-end encryption
- HIPAA compliance
- zero retention
- guaranteed deletion from external providers
- anonymous use for authenticated features

## Data-Minimization Recommendations

- Keep Flare Plan actions short and practical.
- Avoid adding diagnoses, legal details, financial details, medication details, addresses, or other highly sensitive information unless it is truly necessary for your own use.
- Avoid including confidential information about other people.
- Use a GroupMe group whose membership you understand.
- Review the saved message and selected group carefully before testing.

If you believe something was saved, shown, or sent in the wrong way, contact `[SUPPORT EMAIL OR CHANNEL]` and say that it is a privacy concern. Include the approximate date and time and a brief description of what happened.

You can ask to leave the test or request help concerning private-test data through `[SUPPORT EMAIL OR CHANNEL]`. Some V0 handling may still be manual, and deletion or export behavior may not yet be self-service.
