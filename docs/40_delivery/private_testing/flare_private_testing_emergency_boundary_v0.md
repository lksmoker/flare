# Flare Private Testing Emergency Boundary V0

Flare is not an emergency service.

Flare does not:

- contact emergency services
- monitor you
- detect emergencies
- guarantee message delivery
- guarantee that anyone sees or responds
- provide crisis counseling
- provide medical advice
- provide diagnosis
- provide treatment

Flare must not be your only way to obtain urgent help.

If you may be in immediate danger or need urgent help, contact local emergency services or another appropriate source of immediate assistance directly. Do not wait for a Flare or GroupMe response.
