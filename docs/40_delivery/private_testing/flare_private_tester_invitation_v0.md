Hi,

I’d like to invite you to a small, invitation-only private test of Flare.

Flare is a personal support tool for difficult moments. It helps a tester prepare a short Flare Plan, keep personal reminder content available, and optionally send one predefined support message to a chosen GroupMe group.

This private test is meant to help us learn whether the current product is understandable, honest about its limits, and reliable enough for a very small first cohort. The software is unfinished and may still contain defects, confusing wording, or behavior that changes during testing.

What to expect:

- Onboarding usually takes about 10 to 20 minutes.
- Participation is voluntary, and you can stop at any time.
- Testers must be 18 or older.
- Flare is not an emergency service and must not be your only way to get urgent help.
- Useful feedback includes confusing screens, sign-in trouble, setup friction, wrong-message risks, delivery failures, and anything that makes the product feel misleading or hard to trust.
