# Flare Private Testing Known Limitations V0

**Operator preflight:** review this file against the approved build before sending.

## Current Private-Test Limits

- Flare is unfinished private-test software and may contain defects or confusing behavior.
- Access is invitation-only and may be paused or removed.
- There is no production uptime guarantee.
- Support is best-effort, not 24/7.
- Authentication sessions or sign-in links may expire.
- Supported environments are limited to the currently approved private-test setup.
- GroupMe is optional.
- Only one GroupMe destination is supported at a time.
- GroupMe delivery may fail even when the local Flare event succeeds.
- Delivery success does not prove that any person saw, understood, or responded to the message.
- Flare does not read GroupMe replies.
- Flare does not provide read receipts, acknowledgement tracking, or reply monitoring.
- Flare does not escalate to another person, group, or service.
- Flare does not contact emergency services.
- Flare does not dispatch help.
- Local Flare behavior is separate from external GroupMe delivery.
- Your Flare Plan may be empty or very short.
- History, deletion, export, cross-device behavior, and account-recovery behavior may not match a final production product.
- Private-test data may need to be reset before a later release.
- Screens, wording, and behavior may change during the test.

## Explicitly Deferred From Private Testing V0

- supporter accounts
- reply monitoring
- read receipts
- escalation
- multiple support groups
- in-app developer announcements
- automated post-Flare follow-up
- public distribution
- billing
- broad analytics
- final polished export or deletion UX

## What Not To Assume

Do not assume that:

- a successful delivery result means someone is available
- silence in Flare means no one replied in GroupMe
- current settings will remain unchanged for the rest of the test
- leaving the test automatically removes already-sent GroupMe messages from GroupMe
