# Flare V0 Release Positioning

## What Flare V0 does

Flare V0 is a self-support and reflection tool for moments when a user wants
help interrupting an urge and remembering their reasons.

In V0, Flare can:

- let a user define a `Behavior Pattern`
- let a user save an `Anchor Note`
- let a user `Send Flare` to mark a moment and open self-support copy
- let a user save a lightweight `Checkpoint / Reflection`
- let a user review, search, archive, and restore past `Flare Events`

## What Flare V0 does not do

Flare V0 does not:

- monitor a user in real time
- detect crisis state or urgent risk
- contact emergency services
- contact supporters on the user's behalf
- replace therapy, counseling, or medical care
- provide medical advice
- guarantee relapse prevention or behavior change
- claim clinical effectiveness or validated outcomes

If a user may hurt themselves or someone else, or needs immediate help, they
should contact local emergency services or a crisis hotline now.

## Known V0 limitations

- No crisis monitoring
- No emergency escalation
- No supporter messaging yet
- No push/SMS alerts yet
- No clinical validation claims
- No provider/clinician dashboard
- No advanced analytics
- No data export/import yet
- Limited mobile polish before full release hardening

## Privacy and safety assumptions

- Flare is designed as a self-directed tool, not a supervised service.
- Users should assume entries may contain sensitive personal notes and should
  handle device access accordingly.
- V0 should use calm, non-clinical language and should not imply diagnosis,
  treatment, or guaranteed safety outcomes.
- Setup, flare, and reflection flows should avoid promising outreach,
  monitoring, or intervention that the app does not actually perform.

## Pre-launch release notes

### Included in V0

- Mobile-first Flare flow with `Flare`, `History`, and `Customize`
- Editable `Behavior Pattern` and `Anchor Note` setup
- `Send Flare` to create a flare event and open self-support copy immediately
- Lightweight `Checkpoint / Reflection`
- History search plus archive/restore for saved events
- Signed-in persistence for supported setup and event data

### Not included in V0

- Live monitoring or relapse detection
- Emergency response or crisis escalation
- Supporter messaging or support-group workflows
- Push, SMS, or notification delivery
- Clinical scoring, provider tools, or treatment claims
- Data portability, advanced reporting, or analytics dashboards
